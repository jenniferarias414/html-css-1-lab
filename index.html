It's all about the Noodies

by: Jenny Arias

Enjoy this poem that I just had to share!

In homes where joy and laughter play, Naked cats, in antics sway. No fur to hide their charm so rare, Quirky joy, beyond compare.


With eyes that gleam, like stars so bright, They prance and dance with pure delight. No shedding mess, just love to share, Essential friends in every lair.


Their purrs, a sweet and cozy tune, A heartwarming, joyous boon. No need for fur, they're snug and spry, Naked cats, a must-have, oh my!
